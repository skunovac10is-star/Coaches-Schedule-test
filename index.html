    Finding your coverage
    If you can't make a clinic, it's on you to find another coach to cover. That's the standard at Legacy.

    Families build their week around court time. When a clinic is short-staffed, the experience drops, families notice, and your fellow pros are left scrambling to fill the gap.

    We work like a doubles team. Leave your half open with no plan and someone else has to sprint to cover. Plan it out, line up the sub, and hand off what the players are working on so the transition is seamless.

    Coaches who handle their coverage are the ones who earn the bigger clinics and private sessions. That's how we protect the player experience and the Legacy name.
